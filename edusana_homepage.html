Problem

Education businesses are losing revenue in three ways.

Most teams already feel this — but work around it instead of fixing it.

01

Low student lifetime value

No structured follow-up, re-enrollment, or upsell system. Students disappear after their course ends.

02

Expensive lead acquisition

Growth depends on ads and referrals. Every new student costs time and money to find and convert.

03

Fragmented operations

CRM, schedules, payments, and communication live in separate tools. Nothing connects.

Students come already matched to your programs.

Students upload their academic profile — grades, test scores, language level, and target country. EduSana matches them to programs they are actually eligible for.

Each match becomes a warm, high-intent lead for education providers in the network.

This system was originally built by one of the founders to solve a personal problem: there was no reliable way to know which international programs were actually accessible. That prototype became the matching engine behind EduSana.
